Bleezer's Ice Cream

Jack Prelustsky (1940)



I am Ebenezer Bleezer,

I run BLEEZER'S ICE CREAM STORE,

there are flavors in my freezer

twenty-eight divine creations

too delicious to resist,

why not do yourself a favor,
